# Process and Reflection Portfolio (PREP)

# What is PREP?

---

The (online / offline) portfolio used to:

- document their inquiry processes
- record communication with their teachers and any expert advisers as needed
- reflect on their thought processes

![image.png](Process%20and%20Reflection%20Portfolio%20(PREP)/de6a4846-655e-4a3e-be96-a53df272bb4b.png)

# How do we use Notion and Zotero as PREP?

---

- Mark your calendars on Notion for important deadlines, plan duration of certain tasks.
    
    `Home > Upcoming events > Add New Event (Open up a window of Notion calender) > Set the duration of event` 
    
    ![image.png](Process%20and%20Reflection%20Portfolio%20(PREP)/cea94f13-12be-4789-b713-6e82d5bbf56a.png)
    
    - **Practice**: Document the following timeline in your Notion calendar
        
        ![image.png](Process%20and%20Reflection%20Portfolio%20(PREP)/image.png)
        

- Make good use of “[小小的老子的请假记录](%E8%AF%B7%E5%81%87%E8%AE%B0%E5%BD%95%2025e20bc766188064860cf7c46544b0f3.md)”
    - Each time when putting up an entry in the form, jot down
        - Reflection on current progress after discussion with the mentor
        - The plan for the next one / two weeks
    - Eventually a list of progress should be generated, helping you monitor your project.
