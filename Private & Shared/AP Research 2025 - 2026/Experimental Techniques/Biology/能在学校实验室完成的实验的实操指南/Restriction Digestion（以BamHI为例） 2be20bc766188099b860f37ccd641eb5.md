# Restriction Digestion（以BamHI为例）

# 所用试剂盒

生工（B528413）

- BamHI-HF: NEB (R3136V, -20℃)
    - Apply to both vector plasmid & insert DNA
    - BamHI切割序列：
        
        ![image.png](Restriction%20Digestion%EF%BC%88%E4%BB%A5BamHI%E4%B8%BA%E4%BE%8B%EF%BC%89/image.png)
        
- Quick CIP: NEB (M0525V, -20℃)
    - Apply to vector plasmid only, to prevent self-ligation.

产品组成：

- BamHI-HF enzyme (250ul, R3136V)
- CutSmart Buffer (250ul, R3136V)
- Quick CIP enzyme (100ul, M0525V)
- CutSmart Buffer (1.25ml, M0525V)

# 自备材料

- 1.5ml离心管
- DNA样品
- ddH2O
- 冰盒
- 移液枪
- 枪头
- 废液缸
- 离心管架
- 37℃培养箱
- 记号笔

# 步骤

1. 反应体系设置（酶必须全程置于冰盒内）：
    
    
    | DNA | Vector: 5ug
    Insert: 3ug |
    | --- | --- |
    | 10x NEB CutSmart Buffer | 5 ul |
    | BamHI | 1 ul |
    | ddH2O | Add up to 50 ul |
    | **Total** | **50 ul** |
    - 注意：若切割对象为plasmid vector，则每1ug DNA加入1ul CIP。
        - 参考资料：https://dx.doi.org/10.17504/protocols.io.7r2hm8e

1. Incubate for 5 - 15 minutes at 37℃.
    - 参考资料：https://static.igem.wiki/teams/4947/wiki/restriction-enzyme-digestion-protocol-bamhi.pdf

1. Stop reaction by heat inactivation for 2 min. (Optional?)
    - 参考资料：https://static.igem.wiki/teams/4947/wiki/restriction-enzyme-digestion-protocol-bamhi.pdf
