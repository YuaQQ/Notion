# Ligation

# 所用试剂盒

NEB (M0202V)

在-20℃

**产品组成**：

- T4 DNA ligase (25ul)
- 10x T4 DNA ligase reaction buffer (1ml)

# 自备材料

- Digested DNA insert & backbone
- 1.5ml离心管
- 移液枪
- 枪头
- 冰盒
- ddH2O
- 记号笔
- 废液缸
- 离心管架

# 步骤

1. 反应体系设置（酶必须全程置于冰盒内）：

| DNA | Vector (4kb): 50ng (0.02 pmol)Insert (1kb): 37.5ng (0.06 pmol) |
| --- | --- |
| 10x T4 Ligase Reaction Buffer | 2 ul |
| T4 DNA ligase | 1 ul |
| ddH2O | Add up to 20 ul |
| **Total** | **20 ul** |
- Use [NEBioCalculator](https://nebiocalculator.neb.com/#!/ligation#!%2F) to calculate molar ratios.
- The T4 DNA Ligase Buffer should be thawed and resuspended at room temperature.

1. Incubate
    1. For cohesive (sticky) ends, incubate at 16°C overnight or room temperature for 10 minutes.
    2. For blunt ends or single base overhangs, incubate at 16°C overnight or room temperature for 2 hours (alternatively, high concentration T4 DNA Ligase can be used in a 10 minute ligation).
    
2. Heat inactivate at 65°C for 10 minutes. (Optional?)

1. Chill on ice and transform 1-5 μl of the reaction into 50 μl competent cells.
