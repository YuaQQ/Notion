# Style Guide

## Which one to choose?

[MLA](Style%20Guide/MLA%2026020bc7661880089428c4b58ae8fc66.md)

Humanities subjects, especially in English studies, modern languages and literatures, comparative literature, literary criticism, media studies, cultural studies, and related disciplines. ([Source Page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MLA_Handbook))

[APA](Style%20Guide/APA%2026020bc7661880859fb8ddc2d3733daa.md)

Commonly used for citing sources within the field of behavioral and social sciences, including sociology, education, nursing, criminal justice, anthropology, and psychology. ([Source Page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/APA_style))

Also used in sciences.

[AMA](Style%20Guide/AMA%2026020bc7661880f9b862f3ff12de75c1.md)

Primarily used to cite reference in medical and health sciences disciplines. ([Source Page](https://hpu.libguides.com/styleguides/AMA))

[Chicago](Style%20Guide/Chicago%2026020bc76618807e8686e3613e023aee.md)

Generally used by business, history, and the fine arts. ([Source Page](https://pitt.libguides.com/citationhelp))
