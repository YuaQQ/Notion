# Introduction

---

- How serious is ovarian cancer → CA125 as “golden-standard” diagnostic marker
    - Healthy individuals: 35 U/mL
- Simple, rapid, stabilized methods for CA125 detection required for ovarian cancer monitoring
    - Current methods:
        - fluorescence spectroscopy
        - luminescence resonance energy transfer
        - electrochemiluminescence
        - electrochemistry
            - Strength: Simple, speed, low cost, high sensitivity
            - Limitation: single electrochemical signal ~ analytes → susceptible to interference from background noise
            - Ratiometric electrochemical sensing
                - dual-signal output → internal correction → accuracy & stability
                - E.g.
                    - ferrocene-carboxyl (Fc-COOH)
                    - thionine (Thi)
                - Previous work: Fc-COOH & Thi-based sensors detected carcinoembryonic antigen
                - Nanomaterials can enhance electrochemical immunosensor’s sensitivity
                    - E.g. reduced graphene oxide (rGO)
                        - large SA
                        - electro conductivity
                        - biocompatibility
                        - limitation: aggregation → reduced SA
                    - E.g. multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCTs) → rGO/MWCNT
                        - prevent aggregation of graphene sheets → allows larger SA & conductivity
                        - -COOH on MWCNT can link to -NH2 of antibodies o immunosensor
                - Metal-organic framework (MOF) is good matrix for adsorption
                    - E.g. UiO-66
                        - adsorb analytes / loaded electroactive molecules with high efficiency, enhancing sensor performance
                    - E.g. UIO-66-NH2
                        - increase binding sites, further increasing loading capacity of target molecule → enhance electrochemical signal
- Gap
    - UIO-66-NH2 + Fc-COOH → ratiometric electrochemical sensor with higher performance?
    - Use this immunosensor to detect CA125?

# Results & Discussion

---

- Characterization of materials
    - SEM + TEM: micromorphology of materials (Figure 1)
        - Increased SA!
    - X-ray diffraction & FTIR analysis → successful synthesis of UiO-66-NH2 (Figure 2a)
    - FTIR → Fc-COOH successfully adsorbed onto UiO-66-NH2 → successful syntesis of UiO-66-Fc components
    
- Electrochemical performance of materials
    - DPV test of:
        - thionine
        - rGO
        - 3DrGO-MWCNT
        - 3DrGO-MWCNT-Thi
        - Figure 3 shows 3DrGO-MWCNT-Thi having a distinct & amplified redox peak → 3DrGO-MWCNT can load more thionine & amplify the electrical signal
    - DPV evaluation of UiO-66-NH2’s electrical signal generation
        - UiO-66-Fc has increased redox peak → effective carrier for enhancing electrical signal by loading & adsorbing small molecules
        
- Electrochemical behavior of the ratio immunosensor （sandwich ratio electrochemical immunosensor）
    - DPV, EIS, & CV techniques
    - Fig 3C: DPV signals of different modified electrodes
        - 3DrGO-MWCNT-Thi → strong oxidation peak at -0.2V
            - Rationale: the conductive 3D frame structure enhancing the Thi electrochemical correspondance
            - Protein hinder electron transfer → when the electrode binds to Ab, the oxidation current peak decreases
        - When the modified electrode was further bound to UiO-66-Fc-Ab2 → further reduction of -0.2V peak & emergence of 0.25V peak
            - Rationale
                - Further impede electron transfer on electrode surface
                - UiO-66-NH2 has large SA, loading lots of Fc → characteristic peak at 0.25V
    - Fig 4D: EIS signals of modified electrodes
        - 3DrGO-MWCNT-Thi: good conductivity & faster electrochemical correspondance
        - After Ab1 coating, antigen incubation & UiO-66-Fc-Ab2 application → signal change indicating conductivity decrease
    - Fig S2: CV curves
        - Similar results indicating the successful establishment of the ratio immunosensor

- Optimization of the conditions
    - pH value of PBS
        - Optimal = 6.0: redox peak current of Thi minimized while for Fc-COOH maximized → smallest ratio
    - Concentrations of 3DrGO-MWCNT-Thi
        - Optimal = 0.75mg/mL: largest oxidation peak
    - Concentrations of UiO-66-Fc
        - Optimal = 1.5mg/mL: highest redox peak
    - Incubation time
        - Optimal = 40 min: current of Thi & Fc-COOH both plateaued
    - Performance analysis under optimized conditions
        - Linear regression model: I(Thi)/IFc = -10log[CA125]+23, covering 0.01U/mL to 80U/mL CA125
        - Discuss: The ratiometric electrochemical strategy improved stability
            - 3DrGO-MWCNTs form a network structure increasing SA & improving the Thi & Ab1 loading & electrode conductivity
            - the outstanding adsorption ability on UIO-66-NH2 amplified the electrochemical signals of Fc-COOH
            
- Specificity, reproducibility & stability of the immunosensor
    - Use BSA, HRP, other tumor markers as interferences (at different concentrations)
        - DPV as measurements
        - Fig 6a: no significant changes in peak currents of Tni & Fc → anti-interference capabilities
    - Reproducibility test
        - 5 electrodes, DPV method, same 0.01U/mL CA125, optimized conditions
        - Fig 6B: Each electrode produces similar RSD values → good reproducibility
    - Stability test
        - DPV method, 0.1U/mL CA125, electrodes stored for different durations (1 to 5 days)
        - Figure 6C: After 5 days of storage, retained 94%-96% initial signal → satisfactory stability
        
- Accuracy Evaluation
    - Spiked recovery (往现有sampe里加入各种浓度的CA125看所测结果是否线性增加，以及RSD value)
        - Recoveries: 96.80% - 105.21%
        - RSD: < 3.72%
        - High accuracy → potential application in clinical analysis quantitatively
        
- Limitations
    - Preparation uses drop-coating method: hinders mass production & limit application
    - Sandwich-type immune complexes rely on two-step incubation & washing process → long time, cumbersome operation

    - Lots of reactive groups of UIO-66-NH2 immobilize more Fc-COOH & Ab2, amplifying the signal.
