- Fabrication Parameters
    - Explanation for failure preparing sol-gel before spinning following the protocol cited
        - Difference in the electrospinning machine

    - Try different levels of 1% HCl in the mixture for optimal condition (Table 1)
        - Reasoning: HCl is the catalyst; key for monitoring the degree of crosslinking in the given amount of time.
        - Optimal flow rate & voltage for electrospinning using different concentrations of HCl is shown in Table 1 → 1.4mL to 1.7 mL
    - Flow rate & voltage optimization results for 1.6 mL 1% HCl (Table 2)
        - Discussed the impacts of electrospin stability on jet flow continuity & Taylor cone stability →
            - low flow rate, 1.6mL 1% HCl: thin fiber with large SA to promote detection
            - Flow rate increase: due to increasing degree of crosslinking & viscosity as the electrospinning proceeds
            - Positive voltage increase: to achieve uniform radius fiber without beads
    - Fiber morphology (Figure 6)
        - Fig 6: SEM characterization of nanofiber membranes
            - Explanation for beads formation in AZS-APTES: low viscosity, voltage fluctuation
            - Estimated radius for the fibers
    - Halochromic behavior of fibers (Figure 7) in aqueous phase
        - 说明了一下各材料在不同pH下的颜色变化
        - Special note: the fiber is dissolved at pH >12
            - Reasoning: SiO2’s solubility in strong alkali solution
            - But the fiber is not dissolved in concentrated ammonia solution of pH = 11.6
    - UV-Vis spectrum of fibers in gaseous phase (Figure 8)
        - Apparent color changes
        - AZS-APTES has a larger difference in wavelength if peak absorption of 98nm than MR-APTES of 50 nm → significantly larger color change
    - Reversibility (Figure 9)
        - Both fibers have high reversibility
        - MR-APTES is more reversible for gas detection
        - Reversibility performance decreases after 5 cycles & fiber surface disintegrates
            - Reasoning: fibers lack uniformity & have large beads
    - Dye leaching in concentrated HCl
        - MR and AZS has leached
        - Both conjugated fibers show no detectable leaching by UV-Vis spectroscopy → proving fiber stability
        - The dyes are covalently bonded to & immobilized on the SiO2 fiber.
