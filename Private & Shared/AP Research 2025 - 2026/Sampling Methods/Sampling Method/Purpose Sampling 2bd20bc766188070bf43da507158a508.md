# Purpose Sampling

Core Principle: Subjective selection based on research purpose
Primary Applicable Scenarios: Case studies, qualitative research
Type: Non-Probability

Suitable for exploratory research, qualitative research, pilot testing, or when probability sampling is not feasible. The focus is on deep understanding rather than generalizability.

# Procedure

Researchers intentionally select individuals or cases that can provide the richest, most relevant information, based on the research purpose and their subjective judgment

# Scenario

- Case studies
- Qualitative research

# Example

Studying successful corporate transformations by deliberately selecting several companies that are generally recognized as successful in the industry for in-depth research.
