# Convenience Sampling

Core Principle: Based on ease of access
Primary Applicable Scenarios: Pilot testing, exploratory research
Type: Non-Probability

Suitable for exploratory research, qualitative research, pilot testing, or when probability sampling is not feasible. The focus is on deep understanding rather than generalizability.

# Procedure

 Selecting individuals who are most readily available and easiest to reach

# Scenario

- Pilot testing for exploratory research
- Street intercept interviews
- Online surveys

# Example

Interviewing customers at a mall entrance
