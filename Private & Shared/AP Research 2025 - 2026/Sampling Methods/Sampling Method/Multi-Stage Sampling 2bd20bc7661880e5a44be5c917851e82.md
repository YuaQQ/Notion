# Multi-Stage Sampling

Core Principle: Combines different methods over multiple stages
Primary Applicable Scenarios: Large-scale, complex national or regional surveys
Type: Probability

The findings from the sample need to be generalized to the population, aiming for representativeness and objectivity. Allows for statistical inference.

# Procedure

Combines multiple sampling methods over several stages. Commonly used in large-scale surveys.

# Scenario

Comprehensive national social surveys

# Examples

In comprehensive nation social surveys:

- Stage 1: Stratified sampling to select provinces.
- Stage 2: Cluster sampling to select streets/townships within chosen provinces.
- Stage 3: Systematic sampling to select households within chosen streets
