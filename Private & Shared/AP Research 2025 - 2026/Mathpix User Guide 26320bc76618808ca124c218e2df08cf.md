# Mathpix User Guide

# What is Mathpix?

准确识别打印或手写的数学或化学公式的软件，并转化为Word可输入代码 / LaTeX代码 / 图片。

免费用户每月识别次数为10次。

# Start Using Mathpix

1. Sign up for a Mathpix account.
2. Login and open [this page](https://accounts.mathpix.com/quickstart), download the “Snipping Tool for Desktop” on your computer or the “Snip Mobile App” on your mobile device.
3. Once download is complete, click to open the file. The Setup Wizard will appear on your screen to take you through the installation. Click Next to begin.
4. Here you will have the option to add a Snip shortcut to your desktop (recommended for ease of access!). You can also choose to have Snip open with Windows (recommended if you’ll be Snipping on a regular basis).
5. It will take just a few moments for Snip to install. Once complete, click Finish (leave the box checked if you want to start using Snip right away).
6. If the app is running, you can find it in your system tray. Click the icon to open the app.
7. If you don’t see the Mathpix icon in your system tray, it might be hidden, try clicking the ^ button to expand your system tray.
